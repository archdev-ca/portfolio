About

I have always been fond of computers, even before I saw one. At a young age, I knew exactly what I wanted to be that is why I took up a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology course in college. While my college years were full of bad decisions, I still know I’m going in the right direction because even though I never graduated during my thesis year, my thesis project earned recognition from the school, and I loved doing it.

Having found enjoyment in both frontend and backend development: I have gained more than ten years of experience working as a full-stack web developer, and although I have already moved to a frontend role recently, I still do backend development when working on personal projects.

In the past two years, I worked as a Senior React Developer for FlexisourceIT, where I helped convert clients’ monolithic apps into single-page applications. This improved their applications’ resources and data loading efficiency and promoted client-agnostic API design.
